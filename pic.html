What is the PIC Scheme?

Administered by IRAS in 2011, It is a business grant to encourage local businesses companies, partnership and sole proprietor) to invest in productivity and innovation activities.

PIC Cash Payout & PIC Bonus.

From YAs 2013 to 2015, businesses can also enjoy PIC Bonus, a dollar-for-dollar matching PIC cash payout of up to $15,000. (subject to an overall cap of $15,000 for all 3 YAs combined)

a) 60% cash payout + b) 100% PIC bonus

Qualifying Conditions.

To be eligible for the PIC bonus, businesses must have:

1. Incurred at least $5,000 in PIC-qualifying expenditure during the basis period for the YA in which a PIC Bonus is claimed
2. Have an Active business operating in Singapore
3. A Minimun of 3 local employees, with CPF Contributions.
